About Me

My name is Yeabsira. I am a Software Engineering student at Jimma University with a passion for web development (HTML, CSS, Python). I bring a safety-first, aviation-grade mindset to software engineering, and I use problem-solving to build modern, reliable experiences.

What I do

I build web interfaces with a focus on clean structure and accessibility, using HTML and CSS, and I use Python to strengthen my understanding of logic and data-driven problem solving.

Most importantly, I have been accepted into the Ethiopian Aviation University scholarship program to train as an Aircraft Maintenance Technician (AMT).

That aviation journey shaped how I work: precision, verification, and safety-first habits. In software, I combine that discipline with the creativity of engineering—thinking through failure points, improving quality, and shipping solutions you can trust.

My values

  • Safety-first thinking and meticulous quality checks
  • Precision, documentation, and repeatable processes
  • Problem solving with clarity, structure, and continuous improvement
  • Modern, accessible web design that performs reliably
